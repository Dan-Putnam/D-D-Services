About D+D Services

Providing reliable, professional, and affordable services for your home and outdoor needs in Fargo, ND.

Our Mission

D+D Services was founded by Devan and Danny in the summer of 2025 with a simple goal, to make every home and outdoor space cleaner, safer, and more enjoyable. As a locally owned and proudly built-from-scratch business, we’ve combined our education, experiences, and strong work ethic to provide top-quality services with a personal touch.

From lawn care, cleaning, and snow removal to power washing, dog washing, and more, we do it all with care, affordability, and a smile. We’re young, hardworking, and driven to do things the right way, ensuring our clients are always satisfied. With open availability and a flexible approach, we’re here to make life easier for our community.
